# Nutrition Mateo

## SIMPLE SUMMARY:

- This is normal toddler behavior
- Main risk = low iron
- Too much milk is reducing appetite
- Dark circles may be related to diet -> ask pediatrician to check iron
- Do not force food

## ONLY 5 RULES

- 1. Milk: **16 oz per day only**
  - 8 oz in the morning
  - 8 oz before bed
- 2. Water between meals ONLY
- 3. Fruits is OK, but **small portions**
- 4. Offer **one protein or iron food daily**
- 5. No pressure, no screens at meals
