# Name change for Damaris Passport

Renew passport using [Form DS-82](https://pptform.state.gov/)

## Submit the following documents:

- [-] Form DS-82 (completed and signed)
- [-] Your most recent Passport
- [-] Original or certified copy of your marriage certificate
- [-] One [passport photo](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/passports/how-apply/photos.html)
- [-] [Fees](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/passports/how-apply/fees.html) 
- [-] Send Form DS-82 and supporting documents using USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL to the PO Box listed on Form DS-82
