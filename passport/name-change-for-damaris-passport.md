# Name change for Damaris Passport

Renew passport using [Form DS-82](https://pptform.state.gov/)

## Requirements

- [-] U.S. passport
- [-] Certified copy of marriage certificate
- [-] One passport photo
- [-] Money order for $130
  - Payable to "U.S. Department of State"
  - Memo: "{FullName} {DOB}"

## Submit the following by mail:

- [-] Form DS-82
- [-] Your most recent U.S. passport
- [-] Certified copy of your marriage certificate
- [-] One passport photo 
- [-] Payment of $130 for passport renewal fee
- [-] Send Form DS-82 and supporting documents using USPS, UPS, FedEx, or DHL to:
  National Passport Processing Center  
  Post Office Box 90155  
  Philadelphia, PA 19190-0155
